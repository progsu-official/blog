first event of the semester. the room was full.

![packed room at the spring kickoff](/images/kickoff/kickoff-1.png)

that's the thing about a good kickoff — you can tell immediately whether the semester is going to be something. this one was.

![members connecting at the spring kickoff](/images/kickoff/kickoff-2.png)

new members, returning members, people who had never heard of progsu until a friend dragged them in. everyone left knowing at least a few more people than when they walked in.

that's the point. the semester that followed — mercedes-benz panels, hacklanta, the anthropic workshop, progcast — all of it started here.
