not every competition is about who ships the fastest. this one was about who thinks the clearest.

progsu teamed up with aigt to run an ideathon, a structured pitch competition where teams developed ai-driven concepts from scratch and presented them to judges. the challenge: make it novel, make it grounded, make it matter.

the room brought it. teams came in with ideas spanning healthcare, finance, campus life, and logistics. some were scrappy. some were genuinely surprising. all of them had to defend every assumption in front of people who actually know the space.

this is what early-stage thinking looks like when you take it seriously. the ideathon was the first real test of the semester, and it set the bar for everything that followed.
